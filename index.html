01 / About

The dev behind
the terminal

I'm Sunny Suratiya, a Computer Science Engineering student at Govt Polytechnic Sonipat and a self-driven backend engineer in the making. I don't just write code — I obsess over why systems work the way they do.

Backend development isn't just a skill for me — it's a calling. I'm fascinated by how large-scale systems handle millions of requests, how data flows efficiently through well-designed APIs, and how clean architecture separates maintainable software from spaghetti code.

My growth mindset is non-negotiable. Every day I show up — whether it's grinding through Java internals, understanding system design patterns, or writing in my journal to reflect on what I learned. Discipline over motivation. Consistency over bursts.

My end goal: to architect backend systems that are fast, reliable, and scalable — and to become the kind of engineer who makes hard problems look effortless.
